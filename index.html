About Me

I am a local artist that combines discarded items into beautiful jewelry. I have been working with copper and stainless steel wire for many years. I also enjoy macramé. This craft was passed on by two old sailors, my grandfather and his brother.
My “twist,” macramé with recycled wire.
I’m currently attending Pennsylvania College of Technology I will be graduating in May with the 2014 centennial class!
